# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful community, we commit to making participation in Nexus AI a harassment-free experience for everyone.

We pledge to:

- Be respectful and considerate in speech and actions.
- Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or exclusionary behavior.
- Be mindful of our words and perceptions.
- Show empathy and patience toward other community members.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Violence, threats, or aggressive language.
- Discriminatory jokes, slurs, or exclusionary language.
- Public or private harassment.
- Insults, name-calling, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Nexus AI spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, community chats, and any related communication channels.

## Enforcement

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue and tagging the maintainers, or contact the repository owner directly. Reports will be handled with discretion.

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:

- Warning from maintainers
- Temporary or permanent removal from project spaces
- Closure of offending issues or pull requests
